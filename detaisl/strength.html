    Benefits of Strength Exercise

    • Increases muscle mass and strength
    • Improves bone density
    • Enhances joint flexibility
    • Boosts metabolism and aids in weight control

    Types of Strength Exercises

    • Bodyweight Exercises: Push-ups, squats, lunges, and planks
    • Free Weights: Dumbbell curls, bench presses, and deadlifts
    • Resistance Machines: Machines that target specific muscles, like leg presses and chest presses
    • Resistance Bands: Portable bands that provide tension and resistance for a variety of exercises

    Tips for Getting Started

    • Begin with light weights or bodyweight exercises
    • Maintain proper form to prevent injury
    • Start with 2-3 sessions per week, giving muscles time to recover
