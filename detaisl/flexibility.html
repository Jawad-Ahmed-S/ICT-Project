    Benefits of Flexibility Exercise

    • Improves posture and alignment
    • Increases range of motion in joints
    • Reduces muscle tension and soreness
    • Decreases risk of injury
    • Promotes relaxation and reduces stress

    Types of Flexibility Exercises

    • Static Stretching: Holding a stretch for a period to lengthen muscles, ideal after workouts
    • Dynamic Stretching: Moving parts of your body through a full range of motion, usually done before workouts
    • Yoga: Combines flexibility and strength exercises to improve balance and posture
    • Pilates: Focuses on core strength and flexibility, enhancing control over movement
    • PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation): Involves stretching and contracting muscles to increase flexibility

    Tips for Getting Started

    • Warm up before stretching to avoid injury
    • Hold each stretch for 15-30 seconds without bouncing
    • Focus on breathing deeply and relaxing into each stretch
    • Practice consistently for gradual improvement
    • Listen to your body and avoid overstretching
